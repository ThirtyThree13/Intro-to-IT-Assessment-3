Overview


The main purpose of this project is to create a marketable solution to a variety of clients. The overall outcome of this project is to a. have a complete solution to take to market, b. has some branding and information to pass onto marketing and branding specialists and c. be ready to provide corporate solutions or specific additions to the software for specific major clients. The brought to market solution is expected to have adaptable UI and formatting for ease of integration of marketing solutions and as mentioned, for adding specific corporate branding and any additional add ons requested by clients. Our overall goal in creating a calculator that displays memory is to provide the capability and allow the market to let us fall into multiple grooves that will give us a wide, multimarket reach to provide services and later adaptability to what specific clients ask for when we have established ourselves in the market. The larger we can scale our business the more market share we will have and will give us a flow on effect to find new business in new areas that we may not have any application in yet. Working industry by industry will be the best way for us to grow, it will curb the rate we grow at but will sustain and make sure we don’t burn out in industries that we have moved into. Growing like this will also give us key market partners to tailor our solutions for multiple areas of application and give us credibility in each industry as we grow our individual brand and that of those who we partner with / provide solutions to.
